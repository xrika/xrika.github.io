Go Casual

Go Casual is a mobile app that connects casual workers and employers in hospitality through a smart, streamlined platform. Workers can manage profiles, availability, and job preferences, while employers can pre-set roles, filter candidates, and manage compliance. It’s built around user stories that follow the INVEST principles.
